Socca Flatbread with Roasted Mediterranean Vegetables

Description

This easy flatbread is made with chickpea flour, which is high in protein and fiber. It requires no kneading- you simply add water to it and leave for 30 minutes! It is fried in a similar way as pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 9 oz chickpea flour
  • A pinch of salt
  • 14 fl oz water
  • Pinch of bicarbonate of soda (or baking powder)
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 1 red or yellow pepper, deseeded and sliced
  • 1 courgette, sliced of the diagonal
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for cooking
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 tbsp pesto
  • 2-3 large tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • A handful of black olives
  • 3½ oz mozzarella, sliced

To Serve:

  • A leafy salad

Steps

  1. Place the chickpea flour and salt in a bowl. Gradually whisk in the water (an electric mixer helps). Let batter stand for 30 minutes until it is smooth and thick.
  2. Once thick, stir in the bicarbonate of soda or baking powder.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  4. Toss onion, pepper, courgette slices, and garlic with the olive oil in a large roasting tin so they are well-coated. Roast in the oven for 10 minutes.
  5. Heat a little oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour in a ladleful of batter. Swirl around pan to coat.
  6. Cook batter for 2 minutes, flip, and then cook the other side for 1-2 minutes. Slide onto an oiled baking tray. Repeat to use all the batter.
  7. Spread the pesto over the socca base, then arrange tomato slices and roasted vegetables on top.
  8. Scatter olives and cheese over top.
  9. Bake in oven for 5-7 minutes until the cheese has melted and socca is crisp at the edges. Serve with a leafy salad.
